Activities

Explore Edinburgh through unique experiences — whether you're drawn to history, nature, the arts, or lively festivals.

Arts, Museums & Galleries

Edinburgh Castle Tour

Walk the centuries-old grounds and learn about Scottish royalty and battles at the top of Castle Rock.

Royal Mile Walk

Experience Edinburgh’s medieval spine with street performers, historic closes, and shops full of character.

National Museum of Scotland

Dive into Scottish heritage, science, and culture with interactive exhibits and stunning architecture.

Underground Vaults Tour

Step beneath the city for a spine-chilling journey through Edinburgh's darker, hidden history.

Palace of Holyroodhouse

Visit the Queen's official Scottish residence, steeped in royal intrigue and beautiful gardens.

Outdoor & Scenic

Arthur’s Seat Hike

Hike up an ancient volcano for breathtaking panoramic views of the city and coastline.

Royal Botanic Garden

Stroll through 70 acres of floral beauty, tranquil trails, and exotic plant collections.

Union Canal Boat Ride

Enjoy a peaceful cruise or paddle through this historic canal lined with greenery.

Calton Hill

Iconic skyline views and classical monuments make this a must-see sunset spot.

Dean Village

Wander through this quiet, picturesque neighborhood tucked beside the Water of Leith.

Artistic & Entertainment

Edinburgh Fringe Festival

See the world’s largest arts festival — comedy, drama, music, and street shows galore!

Scottish Ballet

Watch graceful performances in historic venues like the Festival Theatre or Usher Hall.

Scottish National Gallery

Marvel at works by Botticelli, Rembrandt, Turner and Scottish masters — all for free.

Cameo Cinema

Catch a cult classic or indie hit at one of the oldest cinemas still in operation.

Scottish Storytelling Centre

Engage in live storytelling sessions and folk performances in a cozy historic setting.
