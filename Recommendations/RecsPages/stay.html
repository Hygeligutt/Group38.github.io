Places to Stay

Find accommodations that match your budget, comfort, and experience preferences — from historic charm to scenic views.

Affordable & Cozy

Castle Rock Hostel

Right next to Edinburgh Castle, this lively hostel is ideal for backpackers and social travelers with dorms & budget private rooms

The Baxter Hostel

A stylish boutique hostel with a warm, homey feel near Waverley Station an is accompanied by Designer interiors and complimentary breakfast

Haystack Hostel

Minimalist, clean, and perfect for travelers wanting a central base without high prices. Great place for solo digital nomads

Cityroomz Edinburgh

Budget-friendly hotel-style accommodation near Princes Street. A place ideal for tourists who want walkability without splurging

Kick Ass Greyfriars

Fun, funky hostel right near the Greyfriars Kirkyard with modern decor and a social vibe.

Scenic & Serene

The Dunstane Houses

A luxury boutique hotel split across two Victorian townhouses with views, tranquility, calm atmosphere and that Scottish charm.

Prestonfield House

Nestled near Arthur's Seat, this country house hotel feels a world away from the city rush with opulent interiors and serene gardens.

Dalhousie Castle Hostel

A 13th-century castle turned hotel, surrounded by nature and history. Accompanied by a Spa, falconry and woodland walks.

Salisbury Green Hotel & Bistro

Tucked within Pollock Estate, it offers peaceful grounds just minutes from the Royal Mile.

The Bonham Hotel

Nestled on a quiet street in Edinburgh’s West End, this boutique hotel blends tranquil surroundings with elegant Victorian charm.

Luxury & Historic

The Balmoral

Iconic and elegant, with the clock tower watching over Princes Street, Michelin-starred dining and timeless prestige.

Waldorf Astoria - The Caledonian

Former railway station hotel blending old-world grandeur with modern comfort and stunning views of the castle

The Witchery

Sumptuous suites, gothic drama, and rich history right by Edinburgh Castle. With it's Candlelit charm and romantic escape.

Kimpton Charlotte Square

Stylish luxury in interconnected Georgian townhouses overlooking Charlotte Square.

Sheraton Grand Hotel & Spa

Modern luxury meets classic grandeur near Edinburgh Castle, with a rooftop spa.
